Biography

Rajesh Singh is an assistant professor in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai. His research is in the area of soft matter physics. With this principal theme, he has studied problems in statistical physics, stochastic processes, numerical and computational physics, fluid dynamics, biophysics, and Bayesian statistics.
