About

Hi, I am Rajesh Singh. I am a postdoc in the group of Professor Michael E. Cates at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics (DAMTP), University of Cambridge. My research interests include the phoresis and Stokesian hydrodynamics of active and driven colloids, continuum theories of soft matter, and the Bayesian inference of stochastic processes.
