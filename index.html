About

My name is Rajesh Singh. I am an assistant professor in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai. Prior to joining IIT Madras, I was a postdoc in the group of Professor M. E. Cates at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics, University of Cambridge. I obtained my PhD - supervised by Professor R. Adhikari - from the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai.

My research interests are in the area of soft matter physics. With this principal theme, I study problems in statistical mechanics, fluid mechanics, biophysics, and Bayesian statistics. My current focus is on the nonequilibrium statistical mechanics of active and driven systems. Read more on the research page.
