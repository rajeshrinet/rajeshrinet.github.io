About

Rajesh Singh is an assistant professor in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai. His research interests are in the field of theoretical soft condensed matter physics. With this principal theme, his research group at IIT Madras uses statistical mechanics, fluid mechanics, computational physics, and machine learning tools to study the physics of soft, active, and living materials.
