Biography

Rajesh Singh is an assistant professor in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai. His research interests are in the field of theoretical soft condensed matter physics. With this principal theme, he studies problems in statistical mechanics, fluid mechanics, computational physics, biophysics, and Bayesian statistics. His current research focuses on the nonequilibrium statistical mechanics of active and driven systems.
