About

Rajesh Singh is a postdoc in the group of Professor M E Cates at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics, University of Cambridge. His research interests include: phoresis and Stokesian hydrodynamics of colloidal particles, continuum theories of soft matter, and the Bayesian inference of stochastic processes.
