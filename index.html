About

My name is Rajesh Singh. I am an assistant professor in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai. Prior to joining IIT Madras, I was a postdoc in the group of Professor M. E. Cates at DAMTP, University of Cambridge. I obtained my Ph.D. in theoretical physics from The Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai, under the supervision of Professor R. Adhikari.

My research interests are in the area of soft matter physics. With this principal theme, I have studied problems in statistical mechanics, fluid mechanics, biophysics, and Bayesian statistics. My current primary focus is on the nonequilibrium statistical mechanics of the active and driven systems.
